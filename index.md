Overview of Jobs in Rwanda

Rwanda’s job market is growing but remains competitive. Employment is distributed across formal and informal sectors, with a significant number of people working in agriculture and small businesses. However, formal employment is expanding, especially in urban areas like Kigali.

The major drivers of employment in Rwanda include:

Agriculture and agribusiness
ICT and digital economy
Financial services and banking
Construction and real estate
Healthcare and pharmaceuticals
Tourism and hospitality
Manufacturing and mining

The government is also investing heavily in skills development and job creation programs to reduce unemployment and improve workforce readiness.

A key trend in Rwanda is the growing demand for skilled workers, especially in technology, engineering, healthcare, and finance, where employers often struggle to find qualified candidates.
